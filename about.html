Our History

The farm started in the year 2024 with a vision to provide employment first to the owner, then to the immediate community. The farm has grown fast reaching to a capacity of 450 layers, 200 improved kienyeji, over 30 dairy goats, over 80 exotic rabbits among other animals.

Our Mission

To be a leading center for research and innovation in animal breeding, providing farmers with knowledge and high-quality livestock to enhance food sustainability and agricultural success.

Our Vision

Our vision is to grow into a premier farm with 1,000 dairy goats and 50,000 layers, setting the standard for excellence in sustainable livestock farming while empowering farmers with cutting-edge research and best practices.
